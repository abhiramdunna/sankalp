Sankalp ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Sankalp mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

**Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform.**

## 4. Data Security & Protection

### 4.1 Commitment to Your Data Safety

**Your data is completely safe with us. We will NOT use your data anywhere outside the App for any purpose other than serving you.**

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

- **End-to-End Encryption**: Sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Secure Authentication**: Strong password requirements and session management
- **Database Security**: Protected Supabase backend with row-level security policies
- **Regular Audits**: Continuous security monitoring and assessments
- **Access Control**: Strict access controls to prevent unauthorized access
- **Data Isolation**: Your data is kept separate and isolated from other users

### 4.2 What We Do NOT Do

✓ We do NOT sell your data to third parties  
✓ We do NOT share your customer information with anyone  
✓ We do NOT use your business data for marketing purposes  
✓ We do NOT track your personal behavior outside the App  
✓ We do NOT combine your data with other services  
✓ We do NOT use your data for AI training without explicit consent  

## 5. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account
- **Account Deletion**: Upon account deletion, your data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days
- **Backup Data**: Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes
- **Legal Hold**: We may retain data if required by law or for legal proceedings

## 6. Who Has Access to Your Data

### 6.1 Data Access Policy

Your data is accessible only to:

1. **You** - You have full access to your account and all your data
2. **Sankalp Support Team** - Only when you request support (with your explicit permission)
3. **Payment Processors** - Limited to transaction data necessary for payment processing
4. **Google** - For authentication purposes only (no business data shared)
5. **Legal Authorities** - Only if required by law

### 6.2 No Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT share your data with:
- Advertisers or marketing companies
- Data brokers or data aggregators
- Social media platforms
- Analytics firms (beyond necessary App analytics)
- Any other business for their own purposes

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Data Subject Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request and view all personal data we hold about you
- **Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (Right to be Forgotten)
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Withdraw Consent**: Opt-out of optional data collection
- **Objection**: Object to certain uses of your data
- **No Automated Decision-Making**: Not be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing

### 7.2 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@sankalp.app with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

## 9. Children's Privacy
